270115: Protein Folding Problem (2019S)

This seminar covers the process of protein folding and it's impact on physiology. Topics include protein structural motifs, their function, protein assembly, aggregation as well pathologies associated with problems in protein folding. Student are expected to read and discuss primary literature relevant to the subject.

Location: Seminarraum 1 Analytische Chemie 2.OG Boltzmanngasse 1

Schedule: Thursdays at 13:15, starting on 07.03.2019

Instructor: Meder Kamalov

By Meder Kamalov, University of Vienna, 2019. Protein visualization with NGL and PDB.
